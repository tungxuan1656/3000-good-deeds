# Vision and Mission

## Product Identity

3000 Good Deeds is a modern reflection tool designed as a private kindness journal.

It helps users:
- record kind actions in daily life
- reflect daily with honesty
- build a sustainable compassionate habit

## What it is not

- not a social leaderboard
- not a moral scoring platform
- not a place for judging others
- not superstition-driven content

## Vision

Build a world where people cultivate kindness daily through small, consistent actions.

## Mission

Provide a calm, private digital space for honest self-reflection and character growth.

## Core Values

- inward focus over external validation
- humility over performance
- consistency over short-term intensity
- honesty over image

## Anti-goals

- excessive gamification
- guilt-based retention design
- commercializing belief systems
