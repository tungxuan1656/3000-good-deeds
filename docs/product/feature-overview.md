# 3000 Good Deeds Website Feature Overview

## Document Goal

This document provides a **non-technical, product-level overview** of all features the website should include.

Principles:
- No deep technical details.
- Easy to read for product, design, and operations teams.
- Can be used as a scope checklist.

---

## 1) Account & Access

### 1.1 Sign Up and Sign In
- Sign up with email and password.
- Sign in with email and password.
- Sign in with Google.
- Smooth switching between sign-in / sign-up / forgot-password modes.

### 1.2 Forgot Password
- Request a password reset by email.
- Show clear guidance after submitting the reset request.

### 1.3 Session Management
- Keep users signed in so they do not need to log in repeatedly.
- Prompt users to sign in again when the session expires.

### 1.4 Sign Out
- Allow users to sign out from settings or the extended menu.
- Return users to the login screen after sign-out.

---

## 2) Profile & Account Settings

### 2.1 Profile Information
- View basic account information (display name, email).
- Edit display name.

### 2.2 Account Security
- Change password (for email/password accounts).

### 2.3 Account Deletion
- Provide a clear, multi-step account deletion flow.
- Show explicit warnings before final deletion.
- Sign users out after successful deletion.

---

## 3) Kindness Log

### 3.1 Record Kind Deeds
- Quickly add a new kind deed.
- Tag deed type as Body / Speech / Mind.
- Add a short description.
- Select an emotion that reflects the experience.

### 3.2 Manage Entries
- View deeds in timeline format.
- Group entries by day for easier review.
- Edit entries.
- Delete entries.

### 3.3 Usage Experience
- Keep the logging flow simple and low-friction.
- Always provide clear states: empty, loading, error.

---

## 4) Daily Home Experience

### 4.1 Daily Overview
- Show a quick summary of today.
- Provide a clear action to record a deed immediately.

### 4.2 Gentle Starting Point
- Show supportive guidance when users have no data yet.
- Avoid pressure-based or judgmental messaging.

---

## 5) Inner Reflection (Inner)

### 5.1 Random Wisdom Quotes
- Display one reflective quote at a time.
- Let users refresh to get another quote.

### 5.2 Random Kindness Suggestions
- Provide simple, practical kindness suggestions.
- Let users refresh suggestions.

### 5.3 Soul Journal
- Support two writing modes:
  - Gratitude
  - Repentance
- Let users review journal history.
- Support deleting entries according to product policy.

### 5.4 Meditation & Breathing Support
- Include a simple meditation timer.
- Include guided breathing phases (inhale – hold – exhale).

---

## 6) Goals

### 6.1 Goal Setup
- Set goals by period:
  - Weekly
  - Monthly
  - Yearly
- Enable or disable goals based on personal needs.

### 6.2 Progress Tracking
- View current progress for active goals.
- Review goal history from previous periods.

### 6.3 Experience Principles
- Avoid punitive language when goals are not met.
- Keep goals reflective, not competitive.

---

## 7) Personal Stats

### 7.1 Core Metrics
- Total number of recorded deeds.
- Consecutive practice days (streak).
- Weekly/monthly trend view.

### 7.2 Activity Calendar
- Display activity on a calendar for self-reflection.

### 7.3 Presentation Principles
- Focus on personal reflection.
- No rankings or social comparison.

---

## 8) Reminders & Notifications

### 8.1 Reminder Settings
- Enable or disable reminders.
- Set preferred reminder time.

### 8.2 Push Notifications
- Send daily gentle practice reminders.
- Provide clear guidance when notification permission is not granted.

### 8.3 Humane Experience
- Reminders are opt-in.
- No spammy or pressure-inducing notification behavior.

---

## 9) Legal & Support Information

### 9.1 Legal Pages
- Terms of Use page.
- Privacy Policy page.

### 9.2 Support Information
- Show support contact information.
- Show application version information.

---

## 10) Overall Product Experience

### 10.1 Cross-Product UX Principles
- Calm, warm, readable interface.
- Mobile-first experience.
- Clear user feedback in all states (empty, loading, error, success).

### 10.2 Content Principles
- Gentle, non-judgmental voice.
- No gamification language.
- Respect user privacy.

### 10.3 Future Growth (Optional)
- Multi-language support.
- Stronger installable web-app experience.
- Better performance and stability at larger scale.
